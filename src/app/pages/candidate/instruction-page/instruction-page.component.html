Candidate Instruction
  1. Make sure that the picture and the exam number on the computer screen are yours. if they are not yours report to the supervisor.
  2. The examination consists of Three(3) Question Stations and you are expected to attempt all question stations.
  3. The duration of each question station is Ten minutes (10 minutes) and you are to attempt ten questions per question station in any order you wish.
  4. If you are not familiar with the use of a computer mouse, use the A,B,C,D buttons on the keyboard to select an answer and use the N & P buttons on the keyboard to navigate to Next and Previous question
  5. If you are familiar with the mouse you can select, answers to the next questions by clicking on the option, and you can move to any question by clicking the question number at the top of the page.
  6. You are free to skip a question and come back to it later.
  7. Once a candidate submits his/her question station, it is not possible to go back or undo the submission.
  8. If your time is up, the question station will submit itself and you will be logged out.
